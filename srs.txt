System Requirement Specification Document

Title:System requirement specification for online job portal




Objective:  Create an online system for employee recruitment.

Functional 
Requirements:
1)System should be able to create an account of job seeker.
2)System should be able to maintain a database of all the registered users.
3)Job recruiters should be able to view different candidate profiles according to their requirements.
4)The system should provide different login to job seekers,job recuiters,job portal organization.
5)Job portal organization should be able to verify job seekers as well as job recruiters.
6)Job portal should be able to maintain database for job seekers as well as job providers.
